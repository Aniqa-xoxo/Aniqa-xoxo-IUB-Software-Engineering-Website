The Islamia University of Bahawalpur is a public sector university in Bahawalpur, Pakistan, which was originally established by His Highness Nawab Sir Sadiq Muhammad Khan Abbasi - V as the Jamia Abbasia in 1925.

Academics

Our software engineering program features a credit hour system covering programming, software design, and databases. With a rigorous curriculum, tailored education and research opportunities, we prepare students with technical skills and principles for success in the field.
